Roger Waters Biography | Wiki

Roger Waters is an English songwriter, singer, bassist, and composer. He is a co-founder of the progressive rock band Pink Floyd with drummer Nick Mason, keyboardist Richard Wright, and guitarist, singer, and songwriter Syd Barrett. On October 2 and 6, Rogers new concert film, Us + Them was screened at theatres in the U.S. and Internationally.

Roger Waters Age| Birthday

Roger Waters Family

He is the younger of two boys, to Mary (née Whyte; 1913–2009) and Eric Fletcher Waters (1914–1944). His father was a schoolteacher, as well as a devout Christian and a Communist Party member.

Roger Waters Wife

Waters married Judy Trim, his childhood sweetheart in 1969. She was a successful potter who had featured on the gatefold sleeve of the original release of Ummagumma but excised from subsequent CD reissues. The couple, however, divorced in 1975 and had no children. Trim passed on in 2001.

In 1976, Waters married again, this time to Lady Carolyne Christie, the niece of the 3rd Marquess of Zetland: Lawrence Aldred Mervyn Dundas. The two divorced in 1992 and have two children together. His son Harry Waters is a musician who has been playing keyboards with his father’s touring band since 2002, while his daughter, India Waters has worked as a model.

Roger Waters Priscilla Phillips

In 1993, he married Priscilla Phillips with whom he has one son, Jack Fletcher. Their marriage also ended in 2001.

Roger Waters Laurie Durning

He became engaged to Laurie Durning, an actress and filmmaker. The pair got married on 14th January 2012 and filed for divorce in September 2015.

Roger Waters Children

The English songwriter has three children: two sons – Harry Waters and Jack Fletcher and a daughter, India Waters.

Roger Waters Pink Floyd

Pink Floyd was an English rock band that was co-founded by Waters with fellow student friends Syd Barrett on guitar and lead vocals, Nick Mason on drums and Richard Wright on keyboards and vocals, in London in 1965.

Roger Waters Band

The band achieved international acclaim with their progressive and psychedelic music. Distinguished by their use of philosophical lyrics, sonic experimentation, extended compositions, and elaborate live shows. They are one of the most commercially successful and influential groups in popular music history.

Roger Waters Us And Them

Roger Waters Us Them | Concert

On October 2 and 6, Waters new concert film, Us + Them was screened at theatres in the U.S. and Internationally. He continues to share clips from the film. In August, he unveiled a portion of “Us & Them.” Us and Them is a Pink Floyd classic that gave the band the 2017 and 2018 tour and the film name. Us and Them were released in the 1973 album, The Dark Side Of The Moon. Rogers wrote the lyrics while Richard Wright provided the music.

Roger Waters 2020

In the Us and Them World Tour, Waters performed the song, Us and Them in 157 concerts that happened between May 21, 2017, and December 9, 2018.

Us and Them movie will be launched in October. He collaborates with Sean Evans to put the movie together using footage shot at the tour’s visit to Amsterdam’s Ziggo Dome on June 2018.

Roger Waters The Wall

The Wall is a British concert film by Waters.  It is directed by Waters and Sean Evans and captures performances of Waters’ live tour. The wall premiered in the Special Presentations section of the 2014 Toronto International Film Festival. Rogers and director Sean Evans were in attendance and received praise from those in the audience at the album premiere. The design of the concert was mostly from the original concert of the same name that followed the release of the 1979 album, The Wall.
